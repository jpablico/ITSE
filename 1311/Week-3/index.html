Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • Main Ingredients
    1. Spaghetti
    2. Eggs
    3. Pancetta
    4. Parmesan Cheese
  • Spices
    1. Black Pepper
    2. Salt

Preparation Steps

Step Number Description
One Boil the spaghetti
Two Cook the pancetta
Three Beat the eggs in a bowl
Four Combine the spaghetti and pancetta
Five Add the egg mixture and cheese
Six Mix well and serve hot
